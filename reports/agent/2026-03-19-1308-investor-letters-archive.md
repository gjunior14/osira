## 5. Ray Dalio / Bridgewater Associates

**Status**: Partially public -- research pieces on bridgewater.com, extensive LinkedIn presence

His books (especially "Principles for Navigating Big Debt Crises" and "How Countries Go Broke") contain systematic macro frameworks.
